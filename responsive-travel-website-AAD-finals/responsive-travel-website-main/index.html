More About NEXT-Travels

Founded in 2010, NEXT Travels, with CEO Prabhash at the helm, embarked on a mission to become the world's premier personalized travel agency. Starting with Sri Lanka, we quickly gained recognition as a trusted online travel partner for tailored tours. Today, our 200-strong team remains dedicated to personalized travel, ensuring our customers' needs are our top priority.

Our Mission

Our mission at NEXT Travels is to create a memorable, personalised holiday experience for anyone who entrusts us with their vacation. As part of our growth, we also intend on adding more holiday destinations to our list; which will enable us to serve a larger client base.
